About Us

WildBreeze Safaris is a Fully Registered & Licensed Tour & Travel company by the Uganda Tourism Board with its Head Offices located in Uganda, and offers tailer made private safaris in all East African Countries. We have offered a number different trips to different clients as evidenced from the the reviews on trip advisor. We handle all trips in Uganda and soon opening up a branch in Rwanda We also work with a number trusted agents with in different East African countries

Membership & Affiliations

We are proud members of a number of tourism association & sustainable tourism organizations and that includes;

TUGATA: The Uganda Association for Travel Agents
AUTO: Association of Uganda Tour Operators
NU: Nature Uganda. A conservation company in Uganda aimed at conserving Nature, Wild Life & Birds
UTB: Uganda Tourism Board. Tourism body in Uganda that is responsible for licensing & handling tourism matters in Uganda
ATTA: Adventure Travel & Tourism Association with head offices in Seattle United States of America.
